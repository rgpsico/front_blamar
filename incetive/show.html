Hotel Description

The Copacabana Palace is the most renowned hotel in Brazil. Since its opening in 1923, this Art Deco masterpiece has welcomed the rich and famous. Located right on Copacabana Beach, it offers sophisticated luxury and impeccable service.

Location: The hotel is strategically situated directly opposite the famous Copacabana Beach, offering guests unparalleled access to one of the world's most celebrated beaches.
